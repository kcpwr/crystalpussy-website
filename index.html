BORN '91 IN POSNAN, POLAND. LIVES AND WORKS IN SWITZERLAND.
As a queer-feminist artist, I delve deep into the symbolism of the vagina, interpreting it as a potent emblem of creativity and knowledge. My artistic vision extends across diverse mediums, including site-specific installations, sculpture, paintings, and live performances. In my work I integrate materials such as plastic, wood, and found objects, I create evocative pieces that resonate with viewers.
I skillfully blend elements of minimalism with hints of kitsch aesthetics and subtle influences from street art, resulting in compositions that captivate and provoke thought. Drawing from my background in art education and therapy, I approach my practice with a profound belief in the transformative power of art and creativity, aiming to ignite introspection and dialogue through my work.
